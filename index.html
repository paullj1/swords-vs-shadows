You are King Geoffrey IV. You have just conquered a surrounding kingdom and captured their castle. However, this castle was once home to the Dark Lord Draco. Draco has merged his forces with the forces of the netherworlds and he has now returned to take back his castle.

The Arch Mage Orlando, the presiding military advisor, realized that this threat was far too great for just one army to handle. He quickly sent word to the surrounding kingdoms that reinforcements were in dire need. However, until they arrived, the castle was left to fend for itself.


Next Wave >

Lord Draco made it clear of his intents. He wanted to reclaim his territory. All that would be slain in the process of retaining it would be done so in the name of demolishing this realm, and starting it anew with him as the omnipotent ruler. The souls of those who perished would be turned into his minions, to enhance the strength of his army of Shadows.


Next Wave >

The Arch Mage had decided it was best not to evacuate the castle. To save the castle and eradicate Draco's minions was not so much a matter of preserving peace and harmony within the kingdom, but also the greater good of humanity, in general. However, how much longer the castle would stand against the forces of Lord Draco was uncertain.


Next Wave >

Your castle has fallen, and the future of this world of light faces a greater struggle than you were skilled enough to eradicate. While humanity's fate remains unknown, the fate of your castle has been decided. Or has it? ...
